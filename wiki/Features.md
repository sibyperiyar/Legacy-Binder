# Features Deep Dive 🌟

## 📔 The Legacy Binder Vault

The core of the application is a completely secure, encrypted vault designed to hold your "Life Admin" documents.

* **Purpose:** Store critical information like Insurance Policies, Investment details, IDs, and Passwords.
* **Heir-Ready:** Designed so your family can handle your affairs seamlessly if you are incapacitated, preventing your assets from becoming "unclaimed."
* **Templates:** Comes with a pre-built template for Indian Civil & Financial documents (fully customizable via JSON).

## 🔒 Zero-Knowledge Security

* **Local Encryption:** Your Legacy Binder is encrypted using **AES-GCM-256** with your Master Password.
* **Offline First:** The app is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that runs entirely offline. No data is sent to the cloud.

## 🔔 Proactive Protection

* **Expiry Alerts:** The app automatically acts as your personal sentinel. It scans your records to warn you 60 days in advance of passport expiries, policy renewals, or upcoming payments.

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Multi-Member Support

* **Family Management:** Create separate, organized profiles for every member of your family, ensuring all vital documents are consolidated in one master record.
