# Installation & Updates ⬇️

## How to Install

The Legacy Binder is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. You don't need an app store, and it runs entirely locally!

### Desktop (Chrome/Edge)

1. Open the `index.html` file in your browser, or visit the URL where you have hosted the files.
2. Look for the **Install** icon (usually a computer with a down arrow) in the address bar.
3. Click **Install**. The app will now launch in its own isolated window and work completely offline.

### Mobile (Android/iOS)

1. Open the app URL in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS).
2. Tap the browser menu (three dots or share icon).
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen"**.

## How to Update 🔄

If you are running the app locally and a new version is released:

1. Download the latest source files.
2. **Replace** your existing files with the new ones.
3. Open the app and go to **Settings ⚙️**.
4. Click **Force Reload App** to ensure your browser clears its cache and loads the new features. Your local database will remain safe during this process.
