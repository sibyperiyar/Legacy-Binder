## 📖 What is this?

In a world of scattered digital footprints, the Legacy Binder serves as your ultimate safety net.

* **Consolidate:** Bring together hidden investments, insurance policies, and critical passwords into one accessible master record.
* **Secure:** Run entirely offline in your browser—**Zero Knowledge** architecture ensures your data stays yours.
* **Protect:** Give your loved ones clarity, not a bureaucratic maze, ensuring your legacy transfers seamlessly to the next generation in case of emergencies.

## 🌟 Key Features

* **Offline-First:** Works without an internet connection (Progressive Web App).
* **Encrypted:** Uses AES-GCM-256 encryption for your entire vault.
* **Biometric Unlock:** Support for Windows Hello / TouchID for seamless access.
* **Emergency Kit:** Downloadable, offline recovery kit for complete peace of mind.
