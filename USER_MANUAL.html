1. Introduction: Why this App?

Legacy Binder is designed to master a critical aspect of your life: Prospective Security (your financial legacy).

📔 The Safety Net

1. Managing Digital Complexity
With our lives spread across various apps, portals, and paperless billing, our financial footprint has become invisible. A legacy binder consolidates these scattered digital threads (passwords, customer IDs, and hidden investments) into a single point of reference.

2. Providing a Safety Net for Your Family
In the event of an emergency, family members are often overwhelmed. Forcing them to navigate a bureaucratic maze is an added burden. This binder acts as a "manual" for your life, allowing loved ones to manage affairs with dignity and clarity.

3. Financial Discipline & "Net Worth" Clarity
Maintaining this planner forces a regular audit of your life. It highlights dormant accounts, upcoming renewals, and your true liabilities, helping you make informed decisions about retirement and estate planning.

4. Protection Against "Information Blackouts"
Technology is fragile. Phones break, accounts get locked. Having a structured record (which you can export/print) ensures your most vital information remains accessible even during technical failures.

5. Seamless Transition of Wealth
Billions of rupees lie in unclaimed accounts because nominees were unaware they existed. By documenting every deed and fund here, we ensure your legacy is successfully transferred to the next generation.

2. Startup Guide 🚀

Welcome! Follow these steps to set up your secure binder.

Step 1: Security First (The Master Key)

When you first open the app, you will be asked to create a Master Password.

  • Why? This password encrypts EVERYTHING in your Binder.
  • Constraint: We cannot recover this for you.
  • Action: Create a strong password. Later you can use the Backup option at the top of the interface to download your 🚑 Emergency Kit.

Step 2: Enable Biometrics (Optional) 👆

If you have a supported device (Windows Hello / TouchID, etc.):

  • After setting your password, look for the option to Enroll Biometrics when you launch the app next time.
  • This allows you to unlock the app with a simple fingerprint or face scan!

3. Using the Legacy Binder 📔

What is it? A private, secure place to organize your life's critical documents (Insurance, Property, IDs).

Key Features

  • 👪 Unlimited Members: Manage records for yourself, spouse, parents, or children without limits. Every new member automatically starts with the 'a Civil & Financial' default template.
  • 📂 Recursive Folders & Fields: Create unlimited sub-folders and add any number and types of additional fields to customize data tracking.
  • 🖱️ Drag and Drop: Easily rearrange sections and folders by dragging and dropping them up or down the tree.
  • 📋 Copy & Paste: Duplicate entire sections quickly by using the copy and paste buttons to save typing.
  • 🔔 Expiry Alerts: Automatically tracks dates (like Passport Expiry) and warns you 60 days in advance.
  • 🔒 Password Masking: Store digital legacy pins securely (masked as ••••••).
  • 🌐 Global Master List: Comes with a pre-loaded template for a Civil & Financial documents.
⚠️ Important: Your data lives in your Browser's Memory. If you clear your history/cache, it will be DELETED. Please click "📤 Backup" regularly to save your Emergency Kit to your computer.

How to Use

  1. Add Member: Click "➕ 👤" to create a profile. It will auto-load the default master list.
  2. Edit Structure:
    • Click "+ Sub-Section" to add nested folders.
    • Click "+ Add Field" to add data points (Text, Date, Password).
    • Use the Copy and Paste icons next to items to duplicate repetitive structures.
    • Use the Drag Handles (☰) to reorder sections.
  3. Search: Click "🔍 Search" to find any policy number or document across ALL family members instantly.

Automatic Upcoming Alerts 🔔

The app automatically scans your binder for urgent dates.

How to trigger an alert?
The system looks for fields that meet two conditions:
  1. Type: The field must be a Date field.
  2. Name: The title must contain one of these keywords:
    • "Expiry" (e.g., Passport Expiry, Expiry of vehicle registration)
    • "Renew" (e.g., Passport Renewal, Renewal Date)
    • "Due" (e.g., Payment Due)
    • "Maturity" (e.g., Insurance Maturity)
Matches within the next 60 days will appear as a notification alert automatically inside a member's binder view.

Visual Template Editing (Advanced) 🛠️

Want to change the default list for everyone?

  1. Create or select a family member.
  2. Design your perfect folder structure in the app by adding, arranging, and configuring sections and fields exactly how you want them.
  3. Click "💾 Tpl" (Save Template) in the top menu.
  4. This downloads a file named binder_template.js which now contains your custom blueprint.
  5. Replace the default binder_template.js file in your app installation folder with this newly downloaded one.
  6. Now, every new family member you add to the binder will automatically be pre-loaded with YOUR custom structure!

4. Security, Encryption & Recovery 🔐

Zero-Knowledge Architecture:

Your Legacy Binder is encrypted with military-grade AES-GCM-256 using your Master Password.

  • We cannot see your data.
  • Your password is never sent to any server.
  • The data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly download an encrypted backup.
  • CRITICAL: If you lose your password AND you don't have an Emergency Kit, your data is lost forever. There is no "Forgot Password" link on the internet.

App Lock Screen

You can lock the entire application to prevent unauthorized access on a shared device.

  • Auto-Lock: The app locks automatically when you close the tab.
  • Manual Lock: You can quickly lock the app directly through your browser or by refreshing the page.

👆 Biometric Unlock (Windows Hello / TouchID)

Enjoy password-free access while keeping your data encrypted!

  • How to Enable: If your device supports secure unlock (like Windows Hello or TouchID), the app will prompt you with ✨ ENABLE BIOMETRICS? ✨ after you successfully log in. Click OK to link your biometric profile.
  • How it works: We use your device's secure hardware (WebAuthn/TPM) to safely store and retrieve your encryption key when you scan your fingerprint or face.
  • Requirements: The app must be served over a secure connection (HTTPS) or localhost. It will NOT work if you just open the file directly (file://).

🚑 Secure Backups & Recovery

Because there is no cloud sync or email reset, you MUST handle your own "Plan B". The app offers two types of backups.

1. Creating a Backup (The "Golden Key")

Inside the app dashboard, you can create a full, encrypted backup of all family members and their folders at any time.

  1. Click "📤 Backup" in the main header menu.
  2. This downloads a special encrypted JSON file (e.g., locomotion_binder_backup_[Date].json).
  3. Includes: The entire database structure in a securely encrypted format. It CANNOT be read without your Master Password.
  4. Note: You can also download an "Emergency Kit HTML" from the lock screen, which includes a visual copy of your password. Do NOT email the Emergency Kit to yourself unencrypted.

2. How to Restore Data

If you switch devices, clear your browser cache, or want to roll back to a previous state, use the Restore tools.

  • From Inside the App: If you are logged in, click the "📥 Restore" button from the top menu, then upload your encrypted JSON backup. The app will immediately ingest the full database and refresh your view.
  • From the Lock Screen: If you are locked out or on a fresh device, click "♻️ Restore with Kit" on the lock screen. You can upload either the encrypted JSON File or the Emergency Kit HTML. The app will automatically unlock, reset your Master Password to match the backup, and restore your data.

3. Factory Reset (I Forgot My Password!)

If you completely lose your password and have no Emergency Kit, your data is inaccessible. To make the app usable again:

  1. Click "🗑️ Reset App" on the Lock Screen.
  2. You must type your current password (if known) or the hidden override string to start fresh.
  3. Accept the warnings to DELETE ALL DATA.
  4. This wipes the encrypted database so you can start fresh.

5. App Updates 🔄

You can check if there's a newer version of the Legacy Binder directly from the app.

How to Check for Updates

  1. Inside the unlocked app, click the ⚙️ Settings button in the top right header menu.
  2. The Settings Modal will appear showing your current Version (e.g., Version 1.0.0).
  3. Click the blue "Check for Updates" button.
  4. The app will securely check the live Zetozone server for a new version. If one is found, you will be prompted to download it. If not, it will confirm you are on the latest version.
Custom Server Overrides (Advanced)
If you clone or host this app yourself, you can type your own version.json hosting URL into the Update Server URL box in the Settings modal to redirect update checks to your own server.

Force Reloading

If you recently uploaded new files to your server but your browser isn't showing them yet due to aggressive caching, click the "🔄 Force Reload App" button at the bottom of the Settings Modal. This forces the browser to fetch the freshest scripts without deleting your encrypted database.

6. Troubleshooting 🔧

If the app ever feels "stuck" or you notice an issue with display:

  • Refresh the page. Your data is stored securely in the browser's Local Storage so it will load back right away once you type your password.
  • Ensure you are opening the app via a local secure context or web server if biometrics features are failing.
